diet-opt

Cost-minimizing nutritional diet over a 610-food pricing table (Kroger + TFP).

Your profile
DRI bounds are scaled to the profile above. Zip code determines which Kroger market prices to use (requires re-scraping if changed).
Dietary presets
Check any to exclude foods or apply macro constraints. Multiple presets stack.
Disease-state adjustments
Each disease preset activates evidence-based nutritional constraints (e.g. cardiovascular = low fat + anti-inflammatory + low sodium/cholesterol). Multiple stack.
Advanced options
Supplements
When checked, these nutrients are not required from food (assumed supplemented). They still appear in the nutrient table. Unchecking forces the diet to meet them from food alone — may significantly increase cost.
Portion constraint
A food in the weekly diet must contribute at least this much on days it appears, or not be served that day (MILP semi-continuous). Default: 30g.
Custom exclusions
Manual nutrient overrides
Override any nutrient bound. These take highest priority — they override profile scaling, disease presets, and all other settings.
